Tips for Market Setup Success


  1. Plan Ahead

    2. It’s a night market and you need lighting, set up at night and use your lights to ensure they highlight your work in a way that will draw customers to your stall.

  2. Promote Yourself

    3. Learn to stand out, make your product attractable with a reasonable price.

  3. Packed and Ready

    4. Packing your items and display pieces to take to market takes practice but having them packed neatly and concisely will make it easier to travel.

  4. Display

    5. Creating height and balance to your stall.

  5. Stock

    6. Sometimes customers don’t like to ask the price so make sure everything on display in your stall is priced in a discreet yet clear manner.Ensure that your items are priced clearly and that your table is not overcrowded.

  6. Essentials for Market Day

    7. Have some healthy and wholesome snacks with you as well as lots of water. Pack a mini first-aid kit with some band-aids, pain relief, hair bands, safety pins, etc in case of emergencies
